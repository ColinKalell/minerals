Mineral Properties

Physical Properties of MineralsThere are approximately 4000 different minerals, and each of those minerals has a unique set of physical properties. T hese include:
color,
streak,
hardness,
luster,
specific gravity,
cleavage,
fracture,
and many more.
These physical properties are useful for identifying minerals. However, they are much more important in determining the potential industrial uses of the mineral.
